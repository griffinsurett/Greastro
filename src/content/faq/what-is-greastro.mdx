Greastro is a modern static site generator that combines the power of Astro with type-safe content collections. It provides:

- **Type Safety**: Full TypeScript support throughout
- **Content Collections**: Organized, validated content management
- **Dynamic Variants**: Flexible section variants for different layouts
- **Performance**: Static site speed with dynamic capabilities
